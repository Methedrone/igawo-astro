**Who I am**

My name is Izabela Gawłowska-Wolak. I graduated from the University of Economics in Katowice, but my real education came from dealing with German tax offices.

**How it started**

In 2014 I helped my family file their first German tax return. The tax office paid out far more than they expected. I realised then that Polish workers abroad need someone who knows local regulations and speaks their language.

**What sets me apart**

- I am not a large office. You know me by name, and I know your case.
- I reply to messages the same day, even in the evening.
- No jargon. I explain everything as if talking to a neighbour.
- If an office rejects your claim and I know you are right, I write an appeal and fight.

**Education and experience**

- Graduate of the University of Economics in Katowice
- Specialisation in German and Dutch tax law since 2014
- Over 1,200 clients served
- Cooperation with German law firms on insolvency matters

In private life? A mother, lover of good espresso, and long walks. At work — tenacious, precise, and always on your side.
