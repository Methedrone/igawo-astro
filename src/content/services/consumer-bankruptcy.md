Debt does not resolve itself. German consumer insolvency (Verbraucherinsolvenz) allows individuals to become debt-free within 3 years and start over.

Many people are afraid. They have heard that bankruptcy is shameful. In reality, it is a legal instrument that protects against enforcement and offers a chance at a normal life.

**Our services:**

- Financial and debt situation analysis
- Preparation of insolvency petition
- Representation before German courts
- Creditor repayment plan (Insolvenzplan)
- Support throughout the entire procedure (up to 3 years)

The initial consultation is free and confidential. We do not judge; we help.
