Kindergeld is a German monthly child benefit. Many Polish families are unaware they qualify, or have received a rejection letter and do not know they can appeal.

The application process is bureaucratic and forms are in German. Officials frequently request additional documents that were never mentioned initially.

**Our services:**

- Eligibility assessment
- Completion of Zusammenfassender Antrag KG 1–6
- Correspondence with Familienkasse
- Appeals against rejection decisions

If you worked in Germany and have children under 18 (or under 25 if in education), you are likely entitled to approximately EUR 250 per child per month.
