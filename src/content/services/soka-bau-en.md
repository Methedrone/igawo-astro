SOKA-BAU collects holiday and wage compensation contributions for construction workers in Germany. Many workers are unaware they can reclaim these funds after ending employment.

We understand the construction industry documentation requirements and know how to secure payment from SOKA-BAU.

**Services include:**

- Review of employment documents (Lohnabrechnungen, Meldebescheinigungen)
- Application for contribution refunds
- Correspondence with SOKA-BAU
- Statute-of-limitations monitoring (claims expire after 2 years)

Do not delay—claims expire 2 years after the end of employment.
