If you worked in Germany, you likely overpaid tax. We prepare complete German income tax returns (Einkommensteuererklärung) and handle all communication with the Finanzamt on your behalf.

**Services include:**

- Full tax return preparation for current and prior years
- Family tax allowances (Kinderfreibetrag, Freibeträge)
- Corrections and amendments to previous filings
- Direct correspondence with the German tax authorities

We require only your Lohnabrechnungen, Steuer-ID, and any deductible expenses. We handle the rest.
