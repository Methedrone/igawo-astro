question: "How long does a Kindergeld decision take?"
order: 11
---

The Jugendamt has 3 months to decide, but in practice the decision is made within 4-8 weeks. If the documentation is complete and there are no doubts about eligibility, the time may be shorter. When documents are missing or the office has doubts, it sends a request for supplementation, which extends the case by another 2-4 weeks.
