Yes, that's our specialty. Many of our clients worked for example six months in Germany and then in the Netherlands or Belgium. We file tax returns from multiple countries simultaneously and make sure there is no double taxation. Sometimes you can deduct work-related expenses from one country in another country's declaration – we know these tricks.
