It depends on many factors – how much you earned, how long you worked, what work-related expenses you have, and whether you have children. On average, our clients receive between 800 and 3000 euros in refunds. Last year we had a client who worked in Germany for 11 months and received a 4200 euro refund due to high commute and insurance costs.
