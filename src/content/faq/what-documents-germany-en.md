The most important one is the Lohnsteuerbescheinigung – it's the German equivalent of a PIT-11, issued by your employer after the tax year ends. We also need: a copy of your passport or ID, your ELSTAM form (tax identification data), and optionally proof of commute costs, health insurance, or language course invoices if you want to deduct these expenses.
