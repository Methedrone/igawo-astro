The basic ones are: the jaaropgave (the Dutch equivalent of a PIT-11 from your employer), passport copies, your BSN (Dutch citizen service number), and optionally documents confirming commute costs, rent, or health insurance. If you worked through a staffing agency, we sometimes also need your employment contract.
