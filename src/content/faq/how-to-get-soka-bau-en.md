You must submit an application to SOKA-BAU along with documentation: employment certificates (Lohnsteuerbescheinigung or contracts), confirmation of contributions paid by the employer, and a leave application or statement about unused leave. Handling this on your own is complicated because the forms are in German and you need to know the company's registration number. We do this routinely.
