question: "How long does consumer bankruptcy take?"
order: 16
---

Standard proceedings last 3 years. During this time, you must surrender part of your income to repay creditors (the so-called disclosure obligation). However, if you have no income or it is very low, the court may shorten the proceedings to one year, or even 6 months. After this period, all debts are discharged.
