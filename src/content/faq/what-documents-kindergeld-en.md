question: "What documents are needed for Kindergeld?"
order: 10
---

We need: the child's birth certificate (with a sworn translation into German), the parent's passport or ID, proof of residence in Germany (Meldebescheinigung) or the child's residence in Poland, a bank account number in IBAN format, and a Lohnsteuerbescheinigung or employment contract confirming employment in Germany.
