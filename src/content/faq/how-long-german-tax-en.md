question: "How long does a German tax return take?"
order: 1
---

Usually between 2 to 6 weeks from the moment we submit complete documents. During peak season (March-May), tax offices work slower and it can take up to 12 weeks. If any documents are missing, the Finanzamt may send additional questions, extending the process by another 2-4 weeks.
