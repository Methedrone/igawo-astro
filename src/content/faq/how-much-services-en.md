We price each case individually because it depends on complexity. A tax return from one country usually costs between 150 and 400 zloty. Kindergeld is about 300-500 zloty as a one-time fee. Consumer bankruptcy is more expensive – from 2000 to 5000 zloty, but it can be paid in installments. We always quote the price before starting work, with no hidden fees.
