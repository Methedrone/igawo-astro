SOKA-BAU is a German holiday and leave fund for construction workers. Every employer in the construction industry in Germany contributes about 13-15% of gross wages to this fund. If you worked on a construction site in Germany and did not take all your leave, this money is due to you. You can recover it for up to 2 years back.
