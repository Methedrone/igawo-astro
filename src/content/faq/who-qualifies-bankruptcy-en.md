The main conditions are: having a permanent residence or center of vital interests in Germany, inability to pay current obligations (insolvency), and no possibility of reaching an agreement with creditors. You must also prove that you tried to resolve the matter amicably. Not everyone qualifies – we analyze each case individually before submitting the application.
