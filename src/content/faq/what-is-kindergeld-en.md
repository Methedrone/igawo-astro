Kindergeld is a German child benefit paid by the Jugendamt. It is available to parents who work or have worked in Germany, regardless of nationality. The important thing is that the child must be under 18 years old (or under 25 if studying). Even if the child lives in Poland and the parent works in Germany, this benefit is due.
